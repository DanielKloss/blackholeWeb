Black Hole

Blackhole is a strategy game designed by David Bodycombe and broadcast on Youtube by Tom Scott

Blackhole is a strategic game for two players

It is played on a triangular board with 21 spaces

Each player has 10 numbered pieces

Players take it in turns playing pieces in ascending order to the board

At the end of the game there is one space that doesn't contain a piece

This space is the Black Hole

The black hole sucks in all surrounding pieces

Whoever has the smallest total number pulled in is the winner!
